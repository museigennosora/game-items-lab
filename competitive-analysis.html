Key Design Patterns What makes bundle UI work across platforms
Value Communication
Show individual prices crossed out or as badges so users can calculate savings themselves. "Save $X" is more concrete than a % badge alone.
Visual Shorthand
App icon grids (Adobe), avatar rows (Spotify), and storage bars (Google) let users grasp bundle contents without reading text.
Social Anchoring
Labels like "Most Popular" and "Beat the average" use social proof as price anchors. Humble Bundle's tier system is the most overt version of this.
Progression / Unlock
Tiered unlocking (Humble, telecom perks) creates aspiration and urgency. Locked content teases additional value without friction at lower tiers.
